# **App Name**: KM Fitness Club

## Core Features:

- Comprehensive Information Display: Engaging presentation of essential gym information including hero section (heading, subheading, CTAs), About, Trust/Stats, Benefits, Contact details, and Footer, designed for a high-conversion user experience.
- Membership Plans Overview: Clear outlining of Basic, Standard, and Premium membership plans with placeholder pricing and highlighting of the most popular option.
- Trainer Profiles: Showcase of key trainers with their name and specialization (e.g., Weight Loss, Strength, Personal Training) to build trust and expertise.
- Dynamic Content Galleries: A visually appealing grid gallery for gym interior images, equipment, and environment, complemented by genuine member testimonials and a 4.9-star rating display.
- Direct Communication Hub: Seamless integration of click-to-call functionality for the phone number (8693036144) and click-to-chat for WhatsApp (wa.me/918693036144), including a floating WhatsApp CTA for easy access.
- Interactive Location Map: Embedded Google Maps integration to display the gym's location in Kalyan East, Maharashtra, for easy navigation.
- Responsive & Performant Design: Ensure fast loading times, full responsiveness for all devices (mobile-first approach), sticky navigation, and smooth scrolling for an optimal user experience across the site.

## Style Guidelines:

- Scheme: Dark. Primary color (accent/call-to-action): A vibrant, energetic yellow-green (#99CC00), conveying vitality and modernity, contrasting sharply against dark backgrounds.
- Background color: A very dark, subtle greenish-grey (#1B1F17), providing a premium, cinematic base that aligns with the 'high-end gym' aesthetic.
- Accent color (secondary highlights): A bright, slightly desaturated yellow (#E7DA69), providing a luminous secondary highlight for less prominent interactive elements and sub-sections, harmonizing with the primary while offering visual distinction.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a bold, modern, and techy feel, embodying strength and precision.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its objective, neutral, and highly readable qualities, suitable for all content blocks.
- Utilize minimal, clean line-art or glyph-based icons that complement the modern, high-end fitness aesthetic. Avoid overly ornate or playful icons.
- Employ a strong contrast layout with full-screen gym interior imagery for background hero sections, overlays for readability, and a generally cinematic, spacious feel to exude premium quality.
- Implement smooth, subtle scroll animations and element transitions to enhance engagement without detracting from performance or appearing heavy, maintaining a sleek user experience.
